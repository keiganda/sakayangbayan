Jeepney

Jeepney rides will only be one-way per destination and customers cannot book special rides when passengers are below 7 minimum and a maximum of 12.

Motorcycle

Motorcycle rides should only fit one person per ride and destination should only be one way and cannot be changed accordingly.

Taxi

Taxi rides can be changed based on customer. Customers can choose what type of taxi they can book based on number of seaters that will ride. They can book a four-seater ride or six-seater depends on number of passengers and cargo.

Bus

Bus rides could only be one way per destination and drop-off is permanent and customers cannot drop whenever they like. Booking a whole bus ride should be 15 passengers minimum and a maximum of 30 passengers.
